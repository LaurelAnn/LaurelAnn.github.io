About Me

Clear, concise, and effective communication. It's why I adore writing. Putting the final period at the end of well-crafted content is more satisfying to me than finding a perfectly ripe avocado the same afternoon I plan to make guacamole.

Well-written communication can bridge the gap between disparate groups, make other wise confusing subject matter accessible, or build a brand. I enjoy crafting strategic content that considers the end user because I like watching communication connect people.

My true passion has always been to create. I've spent the last three years creating spaces, first an air traffic control tower and most recently, 396 apartments overlooking Lake Union. Now, I'm happy to be pursuing a career in what I love to create most: content.
